Order a certified document

Use this service to order a certified copy of a document in a company's filing history.

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • a debit or credit card

Ordering incorporation documents

Orders for incorporation documents consist of only the memorandum and articles. If these are not available, we will include the IN01 or an equivalent document instead.

You cannot use this service to order:

  • a copy of the certificate of incorporation that was issued when the company was formed. It can only be ordered by calling Companies House on 0303 1234 500.
  • a certificate that confirms company details and can include a statement of good standing (also known as a certificate of good standing). For this, you'll need to use our Order a certificate service.

Ordering a certified document takes around 5 minutes.
